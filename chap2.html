Understanding the Impact

Causes: Human activities like burning fossil fuels, deforestation, and plastic pollution are rapidly degrading our planet. These actions contribute to greenhouse gas emissions, habitat destruction, and irreversible pollution.

Effects: The consequences are severe—rising global temperatures, acid rain damaging ecosystems, and wildlife losing their homes. If left unchecked, these effects will continue to threaten our health and the planet’s future.

Solutions: We must take action by embracing renewable energy, planting more trees, and reducing waste. Every small step counts, and together, we can protect the Earth for future generations.
